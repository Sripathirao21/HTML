About the Indian Premier League

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, officially known as the TATA IPL for sponsorship reasons.

Founded by the BCCI in 2008, the IPL has become one of the most popular and richest cricket leagues in the world, featuring franchise teams representing different cities across India.

Played typically between March and May each year, the IPL combines cricketing excellence with entertainment, attracting talent from all over the globe.

Fun Fact

The IPL was the first sporting event in the world to be broadcast live on YouTube.
